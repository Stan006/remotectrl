1. Overview

RemotePC consists of the Android app, the Windows listener, and this website. The ecosystem is designed for direct peer-to-peer communication over your own local network (LAN).

We do not operate cloud relays or middleman servers. Your data stays on your hardware.

2. What we do not collect

RemotePC does not collect or store:

  • Personal Identifiable Information (PII)
  • Usage telemetry or performance metrics
  • Advertising identifiers or location data
  • In-app analytics of any kind

3. Android app permissions

Permissions are requested only for core functionality:

  • INTERNET: To connect to your PC via local IP.
  • ACCESS_WIFI_STATE: To ensure you are on your home network.
  • Storage (Optional): Required only for the File Transfer feature.

4. Windows listener & local logs

For security auditing, the Windows app maintains a local log file on your hard drive. This file never leaves your machine.

5. Website & Analytics

Our website uses Google Analytics only if you consent via the cookie banner. This data is anonymized and used solely to see how many people visit the page.
