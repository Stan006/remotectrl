Privacy Policy

We built RemotePC to be private by design. There are no accounts, no cloud servers, and no tracking.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect or store analytics, personal identifiers, usage telemetry, advertising identifiers, or crash logs.

Local-Only Communications

The Android app connects directly to your Windows PC over your local network (LAN). Screenshots, system statistics, commands, and file transfers remain within your network unless you explicitly configure otherwise (e.g., manual port forwarding).

Permissions (Android)

  • Network: required for LAN communication with your PC.
  • Storage (optional): used only when you choose a file to send to your PC.

Windows Defender Notices

Our Windows listener may be flagged as a remote-access tool due to its capabilities (keyboard/mouse input, screenshots, file transfer). This is a false positive typical for utilities of this class. You may add an exclusion in Windows Security if necessary.

Third-Party Sharing

We share nothing with third parties. There is no advertising SDK and no analytics SDK.
