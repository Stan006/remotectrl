Known issues
  • On some routers, connections drop after long idle periods (reconnect logic is being improved).
  • File transfer may fail with very long or unusual filenames (especially with emojis).
  • Certain Windows setups keep ghost processes around after closing the listener; the app tries to detect & avoid duplicates.
